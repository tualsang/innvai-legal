# Privacy Policy for Innvai

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2026

## Introduction
Innvai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and discloses your personal information, specifically for managing membership and annual dues for our community organization.

## Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.  **Household & Account Information:**
    * **Members:** When you register, we collect the Head of Household's name, phone number (used for account lookup), membership type (Family or Individual), total family size, and optionally your physical address and email. If registering a family, we also collect the names and titles of your household dependents. 
    * **Admins:** We collect your name and email address for secure account login and management.

2.  **User Content (Submissions):**
    * **Payment Proofs:** We collect the payment amount you specify and images (e.g., Zelle screenshots or photos) that you voluntarily upload to verify your annual membership dues.

3.  **Device Information:**
    * **Push Notification Tokens:** We collect your device's Expo push token to send you important alerts regarding your account status (e.g., payment approvals, denials, or reminders).

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information for the following purposes:
* To establish your household profile and allow you to look up your status using your phone number.
* To verify your annual membership payment status.
* To allow authorized organization administrators to review, approve, or deny your submissions.
* To maintain accurate master records of community membership and dues.
* To send you timely push notifications regarding your submissions.

## Data Storage and Security
* **Storage:** Your data is stored securely on **Supabase** servers (supabase.com), utilizing relational databases with Row Level Security (RLS) and daily backups.
* **Images:** Payment proof screenshots are stored in a secure Supabase storage bucket.
* **Security:** We implement reasonable security measures and encryption to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Data Sharing & Visibility
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third parties. Your information is strictly used for organizational purposes and is visible in the following ways:

* **Authorized Administrators:** Organization leaders and admins can view your full household details, contact information, and payment screenshots to verify your membership. This administrative data is also securely synced to internal Google Sheets for organizational record-keeping.
* **The Community Ledger (App Users):** To foster community participation and transparency, households with approved payments for the current year are displayed on the app's Community Ledger. 
    * *Privacy Safeguard:* The ledger publicly displays the Head of Household's name, family size, and a "Verified" status. The exact dollar amount you paid is kept strictly private **unless** your contribution exceeds $50, in which case the amount is displayed to recognize your elevated support.

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
* Look up and view the current data and payment status attached to your phone number.
* Request correction of inaccurate household data.
* Request deletion of your data (subject to our organization's administrative record-keeping requirements).
* Opt-out of push notifications at any time via your device's native operating system settings.
