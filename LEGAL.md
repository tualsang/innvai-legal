# Privacy Policy for Genvai

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Introduction
Genvai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and discloses your personal information.

## Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

1.  **Account Information:**
    *   **Members:** We collect your name, policy number, and region code provided by your organization administrators.
    *   **Admins:** We collect your name and email address for account management.

2.  **User Content:**
    *   **Payment Proofs:** We collect images (screenshots or photos) that you voluntarily upload to verify your membership payments.

3.  **Device Information:**
    *   **Push Notification Tokens:** We collect your device's push token to send you notifications about your account status (e.g., payment approvals, reminders).

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information for the following purposes:
*   To verify your membership payment status.
*   To allow organization administrators to review and approve your submissions.
*   To send you notifications regarding your account and payments.
*   To maintain records of your membership dues.

## Data Storage and Security
*   **Storage:** Your data is stored securely on **Supabase** servers (supabase.com), which creates daily backups.
*   **Images:** Payment proof images are stored in a secure storage bucket with access restricted to authorized administrators.
*   **Security:** We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your information is only visible to:
*   **Authorized Administrators:** Admins within your specific region or organization who need to verify your payments.
*   **Super Admins:** High-level administrators for system maintenance.

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
*   Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
*   Request correction of inaccurate data.
*   Request deletion of your data (subject to organizational record-keeping requirements).
*   Opt-out of push notifications via your device settings.
